Professor: Ajay D. Kshemkalyani
Department: Computer Science
University: University of Illinois Chicago

Review 1:
Had Prof Ajay for 401 and 450 this semester and received an A in both. I do agree his lectures can be very boring but he is very knowledgeable on the topics and an incredibly fair professor. If you end up with him as your professor for either course, don't panic. As long as you attend lectures and do the work you will be okay. Read the textbook(s).
Rating: 4/5

Review 2:
Clearly knowledgable about topics but the slides were straight from the internet. The homework and slides were conceptual and the exams were mostly theoretical. Professor did very little to prepare us for exams, he told us to do the problems from the textbook to prepare (which we have to pay).
Rating: 2/5

Review 3:
The class is just one very big slideshow. I could do that in my free time. However, I pay to be in a university- there should be standards. His "relative grading" style is an excuse for him to do bare min- so all students could get failing grades 
Rating: 1/5

Review 4:
Didn't really teach. Just read off slides, so nobody attended lecture. Homeworks were ok. Did not offer any learning material outside of the slides which he didn't even make on his own. Only 2 exams that are very hard to prepare for since they cover all course material with no cheat sheet or resources to help prepare and few questions.
Rating: 1/5

Review 5:
Ajay is clearly knowledgeable on the topics he discusses but he's a terrible lecturer. Around 75% of the class stopped showing up to lectures by week 8. Exams are extremely hard with the midterm average being 40% across grads & undergrads. Overall this class lacks structure and if you're not a good self learner, do not take his class.
Rating: 2/5
